What is Botanica Online?

Click to find out!

Put simply, Botanica Online is a live streaming service that brings the garden to the living room.
A live stream is sent straight from a country of your choice, viewing plant also of your choice. One that you have nurtured and cherished as you have watched it sprout and grow strong. All straight from the comfort of your own living room.

How it works

Your first 5 seeds are completely free. 5 seeds that are placed and grown in the facilities that are scattered around the world. This allows a garden to be built from plants that would otherwise not be allowed to be grown together. For example, you may wish for a native seed to be planted in Samoa, China, England, Russia and Australia.
